# Northstar Annual Report AI Investment Excerpt

Northstar increased investment in AI infrastructure, including vector databases, internal knowledge assistants, model evaluation tooling, and data quality programs. The company positions AI as a way to accelerate research workflows and improve decision support.

Northstar states that AI-related capital expenditure will remain disciplined. Management focuses on measurable productivity, trustworthy retrieval, and monitoring of model output quality rather than broad automation without oversight.

The risk section highlights dependency on third-party foundation model providers, security controls for sensitive documents, and the need to keep human experts in review loops for material business decisions.

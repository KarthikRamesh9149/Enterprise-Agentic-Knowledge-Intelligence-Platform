# Contoso Annual Report AI Risk Factors

Contoso describes AI infrastructure as a strategic investment area across data platforms, accelerated computing, model operations, and governance tooling. The company expects AI systems to improve internal productivity and customer analytics.

Risk factors include GPU supply constraints, rising inference costs, data privacy obligations, model accuracy limitations, cybersecurity exposure, and regulatory uncertainty. Management notes that poor AI governance could lead to reputational harm or operational disruption.

The board receives quarterly reporting on AI opportunities and risks. Current priorities include secure model deployment, vendor resilience, auditability, and human oversight for high-impact automated decisions.
